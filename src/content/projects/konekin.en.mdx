The RT-RW Net business is simple on the surface: buy one internet subscription, share it with neighbors, bill monthly. The exhausting part is the operations — remembering who hasn't paid, cutting off the delinquent, reconnecting after payment. Konekin automates that entire cycle.

## What I built

A multi-tenant platform (one reseller = one tenant, data isolated) that handles:

- **Automated billing** — issued monthly, with reminders at D-3, the due date, and D+1
- **Auto-isolation** — a customer's internet cuts off on its own when overdue, reconnects after payment
- **Self-reconnecting payments** — QRIS/transfer triggers a webhook that immediately reactivates the connection
- **Financial reports** — income, arrears, active customers
- **Tiered SaaS subscriptions** — free/basic/pro with server-side enforced limits

## Technical decisions

**Auth and isolation via centralized RADIUS.** The customer's router dials out to a RADIUS server — accept or reject based on payment status, and online sessions can be kicked via CoA (Change of Authorization). Safe behind NAT because the connection originates from the customer side.

**Enforced tiers, not just labels.** Because Konekin has a backend, tier limits (customer count, packages) are checked and revoked in real time on the server — unlike an offline license that, once signed, is hard to revoke.

**Verified end-to-end, not claimed.** Every part is tested over real HTTP: the vertical slice router→RADIUS→API→DB, a full month-long simulation (billing, reminders, isolation, reconnect running idempotently), and HMAC-signed webhooks.

## Outcome

The vertical slice is proven to work as full software without hardware — 6 test suites green through a single verification command. A foundation ready to continue with real FreeRADIUS and a payment gateway.
